League Of Legends Champions

Top Eight!

Katarina

Katarina uses her talents as an assassin for the glory of Noxus, and the continued elevation of her family. While her fervor drives her to ever-greater feats, it can sometimes lead her astray.

Kai'Sa

To survive among predators, Kai'Sa became one. Stranded for years with no hope of rescue, Kai'Sa endured through sheer will and a symbiotic second skin adapted from a living Void creature.

Lucian

Lucian is a grim hunter of undying spirits, pursuing them relentlessly and annihilating them with his twin relic pistols. Consumed by the need to avenge his dead wife.

Teemo

Teemo is a legend among his yordle brothers and sisters in Bandle City. As far as yordles are concerned, there is something just slightly off about him. While Teemo enjoys companionship.

Yasuo

Yuumi

Leaving a lazy life of cuddling and snacks, Yuumi and her best friend, Book, go on a quest to find their Master. Yuumi dishes out heals and shields, escaping enemy attacks and keeping her new friends safe.

Annie

Annie's pyrokinetic magic passively shields her from the cold of winter, being perfectly fine in a short-sleeved dress. If she had to actively use her visible flames to keep warm.

Brand

Start getting fired up for Brand as his Burning Vengeance. He's a real spitfire who is sure to attract all you combustion connoisseurs out there like a moth to a flame. He's sure to spark your interest.
